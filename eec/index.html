    eec packageÂ¶

    This is a suite for polar-form-based complex calcultation written in Julia. The purpose of this package is to supply efficient Julia implementations of solvers for various operations as a supplements to the original function complex.jl in Julia1.

    Note

    julia --color=yes make.jl

    Package FeaturesÂ¶

    eec.jl comes with built-in analysis features, including:

    • : \(r_1 \angle \theta_1 \,^\circ + r_2 \angle \theta_2 \,^\circ\)
    • : \(r_1 \angle \theta_1 \,^\circ - r_2 \angle \theta_2 \,^\circ\)
    • : \(r_1 \angle \theta_1 \,^\circ \times r_2 \angle \theta_2 \,^\circ\)
    • : \(\dfrac{r_1 \angle \theta_1 \,^\circ}{r_2 \angle \theta_2 \,^\circ}\)
    • \(a+b,\,a-b,\,a\times\,b,\,\dfrac{a}{b}\): where \(a,b\) can be a Number or a Complex. If it's a Complex, it can have the rectangular form \(x + y\,{\rm im}\)2 or polar form \(r \angle \theta \,^\circ\).
    • converting rectangular form \(x + y\,{\rm im}\) into polar form \(r \angle \theta \,^\circ\) with function: xy2rs()
    • converting polar form \(r \angle \theta \,^\circ\) into rectangular form \(x + y\,{\rm im}\) with function: rs2xy()
    • show complex in polar form, such as: \(r \angle \theta \,^\circ\)
    • show complex in common form in Electric and Electronics engineering: \(x + y\,{\rm j}\)
    • conj() to get \(r \angle \!-\! \theta \,^\circ\)
